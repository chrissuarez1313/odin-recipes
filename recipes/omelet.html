Omelet Recipe

Description

This is the first paragraph about the omelet. This is an omelet made of spinach. It's primarily eggs, spinach, and cheese.

If you want a healthier option, you can use less cheese or use a low fat cheese. Eggs also are high in cholesterol, though their health is debated. They are a good source of protein, and, if you're worried about cholesterol, just the whites.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (or just egg whites, depending on what you want)
  • Spinach (fresh is better)
  • Your favorite cheese

Steps

  1. Break your eggs and whisk together with cheese. If you want to use just egg whites, separate the yolks.
  2. Mix cheese with eggs, making sure its evenly spread
  3. Pour eggs into pan, cooking until one side is relatively firm
  4. Spread spinach in the still liquid top of the eggs, and then fold and cook until done
  5. Transfer to plate and enjoy!
